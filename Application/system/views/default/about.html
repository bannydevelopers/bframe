Our Story

Banny Company Tanzania Ltd is an IT based company located in Dar es salaam. Previously, the company was located in Arusha Tanzania. Since 2013 the company has widened its operations and became completely competent in designing various ICT infrastructures. The company solves both ICT hardware and software problems and has proven its excellence in performance through various projects. Also the company deals with fire suppression systems.

Since 2019, Banny company has been looking forward to revolutionize in-house TV systems by investing in IPTV technology.
